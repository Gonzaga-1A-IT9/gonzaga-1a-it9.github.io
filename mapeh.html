ABOUT THE WEB DESIGNER


Lian Roi B. Aguilar

Lian Roi Baubo Aguilar is a 15 year old male of Philippine birth, sporting black hair and brownish skin. His parents have been working abroad for most of his life, thus cherishes every moment with them in person. Lian also has a brother who is a few years older than him and is attending AdDU SHS, however, their relationship with each other is rocky due to a few issues and have rarely talked with each other. In regards to his personality, his humor is broken, laughing at the least funny things, he is highly interested in history, primarily ones around warfare be it the Great War, the times the world has gone close to Mutually Assured Destruction or the grave of Empires that is Afghanistan. However, his desires conflict with each other, as one wishes to follow through with school all the way whilst one wishes to exploit any opportunity to laze around, only working when forced, motivated enough, or when he has no other choice, such a behavior has already resulted in him landing himself in summer classes. Unfortunately, it appears that the latter desire is leading. Additionally, his physical fitness is terrible, being several kilos above what the average weight of a 15 year old should be, being unable to even do the mere task of walking the entire Matina Campus without his tendons hurting, or even have them recover as a well-rested sleep, most of these are due to his lazy behavior and quarantine, both of which do not help counter it. Despite his negative behavior and lifestyle, he is willing to treat people with respect to those deserving of it, and kindness to those who need it, pitying the ones in need and respecting the common worker, be it a Security Guard or Law Enforcement Officer having to spend hours standing around protecting the masses, a Janitor having to clean up the mess left by the unruly, a construction worker having to build people's future in the scorching heat or a Teacher having to teach a class of childish students, he treats them with respect and admires them for being able to continue forward regardless of what obstructs them.
